About Me

I am Jacob Cameron Carrell, bequeather of abyssic knowledge, Blood Wizard of the Black Moon Temple, and captain of the Draugr Armada. I am known to draw and play video games in my free time. I live off of Little Caesars Crazy puffs.

Projects:

Black Friday

The first game I ever made with my friends was Black Friday. You play as a humanoid string cheese stick named Mac, and you're tasked with buying milk from a supermarket on black friday. Flying pizzas and sentient pasta noodles serve as the enemies, while the employees help you along your journey.

IronWood

This was the next big project to come after Black Friday. You play as a tree with guns trying to stop a doomsday cult from summoning and ancient evil by slaying every foe in your wake. This one takes lots of inspiration from old school shooters like Doom, Quake, and Unreal.

Ice Age Game

Most games I come up with never get out of the planning stage, on account of me not knowing how to code games. This one would've had you play as a mystical creature made of snow and ice that can dawn different fossilized skulls that would give different abilities. Also an old school shooter like IronWood.

Hatchet Man Game

This one also never made it out of the planning phase. It would've been a third person platforming game with a heavy focus on combat. You'd play as a guy who can throw hatchets extremely fast, with the ability to collect different hatchets with unique abilities as you progress through the game

Jake and the 8 Pages

This one is more of a joke game, but nonetheless, is in progress right now. It's a game in roblox where you play as me filming a Ghost Hunters video in a dark scary forest. Instead of a slender man creature, I get chased around by a giant evil tree. As more levels and areas are made, more monsters will be added into the game.
